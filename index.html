Biography

Naliang Guo is a Ph.D. candidate at the Institute of Geographic Sciences and Natural Resources Research, CAS, under the supervision of Prof. Xing Gao and Prof. Feng Wu. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from China University of Geosciences, Wuhan. His research focuses on water systems, flood risk mechanisms, and agricultural non-point source pollution control.
